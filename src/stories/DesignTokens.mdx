# Design Tokens

 The foundation of our design system. Design tokens are the visual design atoms of the design system ‚Äî specifically named entities that store visual design attributes. We use them in place of hard-coded values to ensure flexibility and unity across all products.

---


## Overview

Design tokens are the single source of truth for design decisions in our system. They include:

- **Colors**: Brand colors, semantic colors, and color scales
- **Spacing**: Consistent spacing values for layouts  
- **Typography**: Font families, sizes, and weights
- **Border Radius**: Corner rounding values
- **Shadows**: Elevation and depth
- **Z-Index**: Layering system
