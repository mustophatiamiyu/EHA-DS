Color distinguishes the eHA brand and ensures a consistent experience across eHA products. When designing digital products, use eHA’s color system to pick colors that go well together and support different looks like light and dark modes.

## Accessibility

All color combinations in our palette meet **WCAG 2.1 Level AA** standards for contrast ratios:

- **Normal text**: 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio
- **Large text** (18pt+): 3:1 minimum contrast ratio
- **UI components**: 3:1 minimum contrast ratio
